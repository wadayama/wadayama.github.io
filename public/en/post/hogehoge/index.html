Tadashi Wadayama was born in Kyoto, Japan, on May 9,1968. He received the B.E., the M.E., and the D.E. degrees from Kyoto Institute of Technology in 1991, 1993 and 1997, respectively. On 1995, he started to work with Faculty of Computer Science and System Engineering, Okayama Prefectural University as a research associate. From April 1999 to March 2000, he stayed in Institute of Experimental Mathematics, Essen University (Germany) as a visiting researcher. On 2004, he moved to Nagoya Institute of Technology as an associate professor. Since 2010, he has been a full professor of Nagoya Institute of Technology. His research interests are in coding theory, information theory, and coding and signal processing for digital communication/storage systems. He is a member of IEICE and IEEE. うまく読めるかな。
